# Week 3 Summary - Policy Framework and RACI

## Topics Covered

### Policy Framework

Document hierarchy:

Policy
↓
Standard
↓
Procedure
↓
Guideline

Purpose:

Provide structure, consistency, and governance across the organization.

---

### Policy

Defines high-level expectations and requirements.

Example:

Information Security Policy

---

### Standard

Defines mandatory requirements.

Example:

Password Standard

---

### Procedure

Provides step-by-step instructions.

Example:

MFA Enrollment Procedure

---

### Guideline

Provides recommendations and best practices.

Example:

Password Manager Guideline

---

### Policy Tree

Policies support multiple standards, procedures, and guidelines.

Example:

Information Security Policy
↓
Access Control Standard
↓
MFA Procedure

---

### RACI Matrix

Responsible:

Performs the work.

Accountable:

Owns the result.

Consulted:

Provides expertise.

Informed:

Receives updates.

---

### Policy Lifecycle

Policy stages:

Draft
↓
Review
↓
Approval
↓
Implementation
↓
Monitoring
↓
Update

---

### Policy Ownership

Responsible for maintaining and updating policy documents.

Typical Owner:

CISO

---

### Risk Ownership

Responsible for managing and monitoring risks.

Typical Owner:

IT Manager or Business Owner

---

## Key Lessons

* Governance requires documentation.
* Governance requires accountability.
* Policies define expectations.
* RACI defines responsibilities.
* Policies must be maintained and updated.
* Risks and policies require clear ownership.
