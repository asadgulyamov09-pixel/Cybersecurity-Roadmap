# What is phishing?
Phishing is fake communication designed to steal information!

# [1] Email Phishing
Fake email
  For example: "Your account was suspended"

---

# [2] Spear Phishing

- admin
- company executive

# [3] SMS Phishing 
Fake SMS
- banking alerts
- fake verification

- Google

- banking sites
