# Threat → Impact → Control Mapping

## Overview

Cybersecurity is not only about blocking attacks.

It is also about understanding:
- business risks;
- operational impact;
- security controls.

Organizations analyze threats to understand how attacks may affect operations, finances, and customer trust.

---

# Threats and Business Impacts

| Threat | Business Impact | Control |
|---|---|---|
| phishing | credential theft | MFA |
| ransomware | operational shutdown | backups |
| DDoS | downtime | CDN |
| insider threat | data leakage | access control |

---

# Likelihood vs Impact

| Threat | Likelihood | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| phishing | high | high |
| ransomware | medium | critical |
| DDoS | medium | medium/high |
| insider threat | low/medium | high |
